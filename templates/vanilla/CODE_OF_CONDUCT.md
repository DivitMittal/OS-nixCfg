# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming and inclusive
experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and its community
- Show empathy toward other community members

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment in any form (public or private)
- Discriminatory jokes, language, or imagery
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Trolling, sustained disruption of discussions, or other conduct that
  a reasonable person would find inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported by opening a private security advisory on this repository or by
contacting the maintainers directly. Maintainers will review and respond to all
reports. They reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban
contributors who violate this policy, and will explain moderation decisions
when appropriate.
